Product Case Study: 0–1 Development of Bajaj Finserv Home Loan Digital Journey

Overview

Led the end-to-end 0–1 development of the Bajaj Finserv Home Loan digital journey—spanning ideation, build, launch, and optimization. Built scalable features across app, web, and internal tools, aligning with compliance, business, and customer needs.

Key Challenges

  • No pre-existing digital journey or form engine for loan origination.
  • Fragmented stakeholder expectations across 3 business verticals and 5+ partners.
  • Manual tracking and lack of personalization in marketing and collections journeys.
  • Technical gaps in branch readiness, caller productivity, and lead allocation logic.

Key Contributions

  • Journey Monitoring & RCA: Setup daily tracking, performed RCA, and prioritized fixes using Azure Boards (25% faster resolutions).
  • Feature Development: Delivered 44 workflows, PRDs, user stories, and MoSCoW prioritization, improving journey efficiency.
  • Salesforce Setup: Implemented loan origination system with API and BRE integration. Trained 300+ sales users.
  • AI & Feedback: Used AI tools for data-driven roadmap and ran UAT/customer feedback sessions, leading to a 15% uplift.
  • Call Center & Collections: Launched Cisco dialer, trained callers, developed collection app with legal staircase.
  • Marketing Enablement: Launched offline & online campaigns with full-funnel tracking from click to disbursement.

Impact

  • 15% increase in form-to-loan journey completion.
  • 25% reduction in bug resolution turnaround time.
  • 20% boost in collections productivity.
  • Centralized digital journey for 3 business lines and partners.
